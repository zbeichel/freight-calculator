Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026  ·  QuickFreightCalc LLC
1. Who We Are

QuickFreightCalc LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website quickfreightcalc.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

Free users

Free users can use the calculator without providing any personal information. We may collect anonymous usage data such as pages visited and features used, but this is not linked to any individual.

Pro subscribers

When you create a Pro account, we collect:

Data Why we collect it
Email address Account login, billing notices, service updates
Company name Displayed on your PDF quotes
Contact name, phone Displayed on your PDF quotes (optional)
MC# / DOT# Displayed on your PDF quotes (optional)
Company logo Displayed on your PDF quotes (optional)
Saved lane profiles Synced across your devices
Quote settings and preferences Saved to your account for convenience

Payment information

We use Stripe to process payments. We never see, store, or have access to your credit card number, CVV, or full payment details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data — see stripe.com/privacy.

Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive the following information from your Google account:

  • Email address — used to create and identify your account
  • Name — used to personalize your account and PDF quotes (you can change this at any time)
  • Profile picture URL — used to display your account avatar (optional)
  • Google account ID — used internally to link your Google login to your QuickFreightCalc account

We do not request access to any other Google services (such as Gmail, Drive, Calendar, or Contacts), and we cannot read or modify any other data in your Google account. You can revoke our access at any time from your Google account settings at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Automatically collected data

Like most websites, we may collect standard server log information including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. This data is used for security and performance purposes and is not linked to your account.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process your subscription and send billing-related communications
  • Populate your PDF quotes with your company information
  • Sync your saved profiles and settings across devices
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve the Service based on usage patterns
  • Send important notices about changes to the Service or these policies

We do not use your information for advertising purposes and we do not send marketing emails without your consent.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for their marketing or commercial purposes. Full stop.

5. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of third-party services to operate the Service:

  • Supabase — stores your account data and profile information securely
  • Stripe — processes subscription payments
  • Netlify — hosts the website
  • Google Maps — calculates mileage between locations
  • EIA (U.S. Energy Information Administration) — provides live diesel price data

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We select services with strong privacy practices and only share the minimum data necessary for them to perform their function.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored securely in Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption and security practices. Data is stored in the United States.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Some of your profile settings (such as display preferences) are also stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers except when you save changes to your account.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct — update your account information at any time from your profile page
  • Delete — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Cancel — cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your profile page

To make a data request or account deletion request, email us at info@quickfreightcalc.com.

8. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We use browser localStorage to save your theme preference (light/dark mode) and calculator settings locally on your device. This is purely for your convenience and does not involve any tracking.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for use by adults in a professional capacity. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify Pro subscribers of material changes via email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

QuickFreightCalc LLC is a Pennsylvania limited liability company. For privacy-related questions or data requests, email us at info@quickfreightcalc.com.
