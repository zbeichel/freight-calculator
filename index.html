QuickFreightCalc is a free freight rate calculator built for freight brokers, truck drivers, owner-operators, and dispatchers. Get instant FTL (full truckload) and LTL (less-than-truckload) spot rate quotes using live DAT Trendlines rates — no account or signup required.

The load profitability calculator helps owner-operators and carriers decide whether a load is worth taking. Enter the offered rate, loaded miles, dead head miles, fuel cost, driver pay, and fixed costs to see net profit, profit per mile, profit per hour, and a clear Accept, Marginal, or Decline verdict.

The LTL calculator uses NMFC freight class with automatic density-based class detection, CWT rate calculation, and a full accessorial charge builder including FSC, liftgate, residential delivery, inside delivery, limited access, appointment fees, and hazmat.

Additional features include a FMCSA-compliant HOS trip planner for estimating driving days and rest stops, live EIA weekly diesel prices by PADD region, seasonal rate multipliers for major produce corridors, and a transit day calculator that flags extended-transit loads and suggests appropriate rate adjustments.

QuickFreightCalc is free to use and works on desktop and mobile. Rates are sourced from DAT Trendlines and updated monthly. For informational use only — always verify before quoting.
